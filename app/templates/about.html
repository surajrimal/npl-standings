About Nepal Premier League 2024

The Nepal Premier League (NPL) 2024 is the inaugural season of Nepalâ€™s premier T20 cricket tournament. It aims to bring top-quality cricket to the country, providing an opportunity for local and international cricketers to showcase their skills. The NPL will run from November 30 to December 21, 2024, featuring 8 teams and 32 matches across several cities in Nepal.

Key Features

  • 8 Competing Teams: Top teams from across Nepal, including Biratnagar Kings, Kathmandu Gurkhas, Pokhara Avengers, and more.
  • 32 Matches: An exciting tournament filled with intense competition, culminating in the grand final.
  • International Talent: The NPL will feature a mix of local heroes and international stars, making it a must-watch event.
  • World-Class Venues: Matches will be played at the renowned Tribhuvan University International Cricket Ground in Kirtipur, Kathmandu.

Vision

The vision of the Nepal Premier League 2024 is to promote cricket in Nepal and provide a platform for emerging players to compete at the highest level. By organizing such a prestigious tournament, we aim to bring global recognition to Nepal's cricket scene.
